Stop & Go Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

Overview

Stop & Go is a reflex and memory game for mobile devices.

This privacy policy explains what information may be handled by the game and by third-party services used in the app.

Data Collection

Stop & Go does not directly collect personal information from users.

The game may store local gameplay data on the device, such as:

  • Best score.
  • Sound setting.
  • Game progress or gameplay preferences.

This local data stays on the device and is used only to improve the gameplay experience.

Advertising

Stop & Go may show banner ads and rewarded ads using Google AdMob.

Google AdMob may collect and process data according to its own privacy policy, such as:

  • Device information.
  • Advertising identifiers.
  • Approximate location.
  • Ad interaction data.
  • Diagnostics and performance data.

Google privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Google AdMob: https://admob.google.com

Rewarded Ads

Stop & Go may offer optional rewarded ads. These ads allow the player to receive an in-game reward, such as a revive, after watching an ad.

Watching rewarded ads is optional.

Children

Stop & Go is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can review the situation.

Data Sharing

Stop & Go does not sell personal information.

Data may be processed by third-party services such as Google AdMob according to their own privacy policies.

Security

We use reasonable measures to keep the app experience safe. However, no digital service can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Changes To This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this document.
